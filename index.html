About Me

Hello! I'm Andre, a junior software engineer located in Western New York. I am part of the Technology Development Program at M&T Bank, gaining exposure to different technology areas in the department. Currently, my work has included scripting and backend development, as well as working with integrations between servers and SaaS applications.

However, my interests lie in systems security and machine learning. Before joining M&T, most of my work experience had been in cybersecurity research, while my past projects touched upon full-stack development and exploring new areas of artificial intelligence. They started me on the path to work in a collaborative environment and constantly develop my skills by trying new techniques and approaches.

Besides those interests, I also hold a strong belief in community. Volunteering and community engagement have been a passion of mine since I was little, and I continue to bring those commitments to M&T Bank and the WNY community. As I build my career, I hope to take pride in developing tools or products useful in improving the lives of others.
